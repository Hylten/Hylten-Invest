## Asset Based Lending as a Foundation of Stability

Asset Based Lending, or ABL, is one of the purest forms of conservative finance.

  

Real assets.

Clear valuation.

Direct collateralization.

Clean lines of responsibility.

  

For families that think in decades rather than quarters, ABL offers a disciplined framework for stability and optionality.

  

Hylten-Invest provides lending across a defined spectrum of assets, always with a conservative approach to loan to value and counterparty risk. Our mandate is not to push leverage. Our mandate is to preserve integrity within the capital structure.

  

ABL allows families to remain invested while still gaining access to liquidity for new opportunities, strategic acquisitions, or internal capital reallocation.

  

The advantage is simple.

  

You do not liquidate.

You do not disrupt long term compounding.

You maintain continuity of your strategic positions.

  

In an environment where markets can reprice within hours, ABL creates a buffer that protects wealth from reactive decision making.
